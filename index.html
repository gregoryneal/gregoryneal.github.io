I'm a multitalented individual who has an interest in learning new things. My interests are wide ranging from electronics, music production, programming, and even camper van conversions. My undergraduate degree is in Math and Physics, awarded to me in 2016 from Sam Houston State University. In 2018 I found employment as a novice carpenter building the interiors of camper vans. Eventually I began doing electrical work. All the while maintaining my hobbies outside of work.

My other hobbies keep me busy outside of work, I like to record, mix and master music at my small recording studio. I also maintain a github page for any programming projects that I found interesting. There are links to both of those at the top.

Professionally I have worked as an all around carpenter/electrician at a camper van conversion shop. There I learned the hands on aspects of doing electrical work as I constructed and installed custom wiring harnesses. I'm knowledgeable with most other aspects of camper van conversion/RV work as well: solar panels, inverters/shore power, air conditioners, gas heaters, flooring, insulation, interior paneling kits, and more.
